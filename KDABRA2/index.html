TITULO KDABRA

KDABRA, arose from the necessity to have a low-cost, all in one, integrated system to manage multimedia in Radio and/or television. Under this concept and making use of the latest tools that new technology has to offer, we've developed KDABRA as a new way of handling radio and television, providing simultaneous video, graphic, audio and mixing management.
